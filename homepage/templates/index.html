What is Startup Week?

Victoria’s startup community is booming and it keeps getting bigger and better! Startup Victoria is really excited about all the great things that are happening in our community and we want everyone to know about it.

To get people as excited as we are they first need to know what is happening in our great community. We believe that through highlighting the major players in the community we can get people excited about our community and hopefully create More & Better Founders in the process.

We hope you want to join us in the pursuit of creating More & Better Founders and showcasing why Melbourne’s startup community is top notch!

Proposed activities:

  • Open-doors at co-working spaces
  • Office hours with Melbourne's successful founders
  • Office hours at the VC's office
  • Lean Startup Melbourne
  • Startup events

You can participate by hosting one of the startup events. You are free to decide of the structure of the event: workshop, guest speaker, networking event, etc.

The goal is to provide attendees with an insight into the startup world, and convince potential founders to get involved and create their own startup (or work for a startup).
