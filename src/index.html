What is Startup Week?

Victoria’s startup community is booming and it keeps getting bigger and better! Startup Victoria is really excited about all the great things that are happening in our community and we want everyone to know about it.

That is why we are running Startup Week, now in its second year. We hope you will join us in celebrating the diversity and breadth of Melbourne’s startup ecosystem.

Startup Week is all about Melbourne’s startup community. The idea is that the more established startups in the community open their doors and show founders what working at and founding a startup is all about. The week will consist of events all over the city, at all the major players in the community.

Some of the activities:

  • Open-doors at co-working spaces
  • Office hours with Melbourne's successful founders
  • Office hours at the VC's office
  • Lean Startup Melbourne
  • Startup events
